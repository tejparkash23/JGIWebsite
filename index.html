Late Giani Balwant Singh
Founder

Mr. Giani Balwant Singh was a great teacher and a great visionary. He is the founder of Janta Senior Secondary School who came in 1957. Giani Balwant Singh wanted to uplift rural areas and believed that proper education could significantly develop rural areas. The goal of our founder was to set educational institutions head to head with excellence and help children from all areas to get proper education so that they can go on with their lives and become successful. He always thought of expanding the education institutions to the next level and was always ready to go the extra mile to achieve his goal.

Ranjit Kaur
Director-General

Ranjit Kaur, our director, has been working for years and striving to provide quality educations to the rural and backward areas. Ranjit Kaur wants to provide education to those girls who cannot get an education due to their parents backward thinking, and she wants to change and empower women's rights to study. Ranjit Kaur always stands committed to the rural masses and communities and works hard so that Janta Group Of Institutions can extend its reach and help all children to get an education and strive to become better people. Janta Groups Of Institutions will work hard to achieve its goal.

S. Dewender Singh
Chairman

The chairman of the Janta Group of institutions, S. Dewender Singh, has provided quality technical and professional education in rural and backward areas. The chairman firmly believed in the three approaches connected to nurture and grow. Janta Group Of Institutions will strive to connect and grow with the people. S. Dewender Singh wants to help those who cannot get an education by offering them professional education, helping those areas become more developed, and taking educational institutions to the next level. S. Dewender Singh is always willing to take extra steps for the development of the country.
